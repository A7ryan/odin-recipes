Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Cover with foil and bake.
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
