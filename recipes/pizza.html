Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 pinch white sugar
  • 3 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • Pizza Toppings

Steps

  1. Make dough.
  2. Carefully place one piece of dough on the hot grill.
  3. Working quickly, brush garlic oil over crust.
  4. Add: tomato sauce, chopped tomatoes, olives, red peppers, cheese, and basil.
  5. Close the lid and cook until cheese melts.
