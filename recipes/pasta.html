Pasta Recipe

Description

Pizza is usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Seasoning mix
  • 1 pinch white sugar
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the pasta.
  2. Whisk the dressing with the seasoning mix.
  3. Combine the pasta with the vegetables, then toss in the seasoned dressing.
